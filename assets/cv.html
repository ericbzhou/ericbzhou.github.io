Research

  • Generative AI, Human Creativity, and Art

    By Eric Zhou, Dokyun Lee
    Status: Under review at PNAS

    SSRN Link

    Abstract: Recent text-to-image AI tools have boosted human creative productivity by 25% and increased artwork value by 50%. While peak content novelty increases, average novelty declines, suggesting an expanding but inefficient idea space. AI-assisted artists who explore novel ideas receive more favorable evaluations. Importantly, AI adoption reduces value concentration among adopters. Ideation and filtering are likely necessary skills, giving rise to "generative synesthesia" - blending human exploration and AI exploitation for new creative workflows.

  • Economics of Image-Based Seller Quality Signals

    By Eric Zhou, Xiang Hui, Dokyun Lee
    Status: In progress...

    Abstract: Certain image signals on eBay can substitute for reputation for sellers with less than 100% positive feedback, increasing conversion rates and achieving a 5% price premium. However, these effects are only significant for less reputable sellers.
