PHILOSOPHY

"How are human and artificial intelligence (AI) co-evolving?"

Recognizing the transformative impacts and inherent risks of AI, I formulate my research agenda and teaching philosophy around equipping society to leverage AI responsibly and revealing its potential to augment human capabilities while accounting for its unintended consequences.

In the classroom, this translates to a commitment to fostering an inclusive and hands-on learning environment that empowers students to harness AI as a tool for enhancing their intellectual capabilities and learning efficiency. I prioritize practical applications of AI in business contexts, from fostering a deeper technical understanding of generative technologies to highlight capabilities and limitations of such tools, to emphasizing ethical considerations and real-world implications. I aim to cultivate critical thinking and adaptability among students, preparing them to navigate the evolving landscape of business technology with confidence and responsibility.
