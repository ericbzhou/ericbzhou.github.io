RESEARCH STATEMENT

My research interests are broadly the application, innovation and impact of AI and frontier technologies on society and in the future of work. In particular, I am interested in:

  • Computational creativity
  • Generative AI
  • Human-AI collaboration
  • Economics of unstructured data

I apply a combination of deep learning, multimodal feature extraction, and causal inference methods to large-scale datasets to derive insights from unstructured data.
